About Aayo Kabadi

Aayo Kabadi is a web application designed to streamline waste management by connecting households and businesses with scrap dealers and waste collectors. Our goal is to reduce the environmental impact of improper waste disposal while empowering communities to adopt sustainable practices.

Key Features

  • Effortless Waste Categorization
  • Sell Scrap Online
  • Dynamic Pricing
  • Convenient Pickup Service
  • Incentive System
  • Environmental Impact Reports
